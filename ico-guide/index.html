Dragonchain Public Token Sale Payment Guide

Hello and thank you for participating in the Dragonchain token sale of Dragons.

First, don’t feel rushed. Take a deep breath. We’ve given you a month to participate.

To take part in the public sale, you will need either BTC or ETH.

You will also need an ERC20 capable wallet that you control (we suggest myetherwallet/MEW).


Bitcoin Payment Instructions

If you’re using BTC to purchase, you will need the MEW address for the payout address (this is where we will send Dragons to you).

You should ideally send your BTC from your own local wallet so that you control the keys If you send from your own wallet you will be able to sign a message or send a transaction to prove to anyone that you own the address from which you’ve contributed.


Ethereum Payment Instructions

If you’re using ETH to purchase, you will need to first move your ETH to your MEW wallet address and then send your ETH to us from that MEW wallet address.

DO NOT SEND YOUR ETHEREUM FROM ANY EXCHANGE OR WALLET THAT YOU HAVE NOT VERIFIED WILL SUPPORT A NEW ERC20 TOKEN
